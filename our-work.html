In October of 2016 we had the opportunity to go to Cuba and have a dance workshop with the church community there. Cuba is a closed off country where the possibility to do evangelism is none existant. They are however a culture that loves and appreciate music and dancing. We were asked to do a dance workshop with the different churches there. All in all we had a little over one hundred people between the ages of six and sixty-three. We had two full days of dancing and fellowship and on the third day we performed on the streets in Havanna with many people walking by and stopping to watch.
