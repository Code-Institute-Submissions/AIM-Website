Always in motion

Who we are

We are a Christian ministry currently based in Sweden that travel worldwide holding dance and drama workshops for churches and youth organisations. We believe that as Christians we should always remain in motion in what God wants us to do. We’ve all been given gifts and we as a ministry want to help utilise young people in the creative gifts that God has given them.

Our focus:

Dance and drama workshops

We hold dance and drama workshops all over the country as well as internationally. In general the workshops consists of learning choreography or dramas but also equip you with the skills to teach others. The workshops can be tailored according to your needs and no workshop is the other one alike.

Performances

At the end of our workshops we always put up a performance. We give you ideas and tips of how to set the stage as well as how to give the best performance you can give.

Teaching

“Let them praise His name with dancing” Psalms 149:3

What does the Bible say about dancing? We believe it’s important as Christians to know why it’s important to dance and be creative. Only then can we go further into God’s plans for us.
